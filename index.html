What's Your Communication Style?

Have you ever met someone to whom you just couldn't get through?

Although there could be many factors contributing to lack of communication, one of the most common reasons is that communication styles differ. All people have a communication style based on a combination of four types: demonstrator, assertor, contemplator, and narrator.

Try this self-quiz to find your communication style and read about the four basic types.
